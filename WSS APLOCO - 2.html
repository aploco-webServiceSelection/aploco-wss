WSS APLOCO Web Service Selection based on the Quality of Interface Design and Quality of Service This web page is a companion to our ICWS 2019 submission entitled "Web Service Selection based on the Quality of Interface Design and Quality of Service".

1. Approach Overview

In this work, we propose a new Web service selection approach that combines QoS parameters and Web service design quality. The proposed approach starts with prefiltring and classification of Web services (WSs) based on established design metrics. Afterward, our approach creates the relative ranking of each WS as a decision-making problem. We adopt logarithmic concept as a new multi-criteria decision analysis method to the eligible Web services. Top-k Web services satisfying the antipatterns and QoS requirements of the end user are selected.
